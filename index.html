by Broderick O'Linn
Published: Jul 19, 2020

I can’t tell if these players absolutely hate the bubble or are enjoying the hell out of being in close proximity to over 300 NBA players. Either way, the NBA told eight teams that they sucked too much throughout the year and won’t have the opportunity to shotgun beers and possibly play eight or more meaningless games of basketball unless you’re a title contender. NBA Power Rankings Heading into the Restart

by Broderick O'Linn
Published: July 7, 2020

This is my comprehensive list on each team ranked heading back into the NBA season. I used a mixture of criteria to put these teams in order:

  1. Looked at where they left off when the season came to an abrupt stop.
  2. Looked at the players they have available for when the season resumes.
  3. Glanced at how the multiple month layoff may have affected each of the teams.
  4. Take a look at their schedule and style of play and how those two could help or hurt them.

Certain criteria will be talked about more for specific teams. Some teams have no chance in hell and will barely be talked about. Others may need long explanations but are ranked particularly low. I even predicted some of the offseason moves for teams that I don’t think have any chance. All will be described in these rankings.
